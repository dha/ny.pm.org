ny.pm
Those who are both New York area Perl programmers and in the know, religiously read and post to the very finest fountain of information, the ny.pm mailing list.
To subscribe to the ny.pm list send mail to majordomo@lists.pm.org with "subscribe ny" in the body of the message.
To post to this list send mail to ny@lists.pm.org

As of Monday the 27th of February, 2006, the mailing list was having some problems. These have now been solved. Thanks for your patience.

Note: ny.pm is a list devoted to the activities of ny.pm. It is not generally a technical list. It is definitely not a help desk or a job forum.

For those New York area Perl progammers in the know and looking for work (or employees), we used to have a New York Perl Jobs Mailing List.
Times, however have changed. The list came to be rarely used and has been deactivated. A big reason for this was the creation of http://jobs.perl.org, which serves as a more general jobs resource and is where you should now look for the things the nyc-perl-jobs list used to do.
