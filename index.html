Welcome to NY.pm

or as we like to say /New York Perl M((o|u)ngers|aniacs)*/

Perl Mongers is a loose confederation of Perl hackers, coders, and accidental programmers whose goal is to develop the social community of Perl - after all, it's more than a language - it's a culture and a floor wax.

No Ordinary Perl Mongers

New York Perl Mongers was the first ever Perl Mongers group, created by brian d foy in 1997 at the First O'Reilly Perl Conference (later to became known as OSCON).

Regular Socal Meetings

NY.pm has "strictly social" meetings approximately once a month. We have been doing so for over 10 years, and you are warmly invited to attend.

There is no set meeting schedule. Instead, the mailing list is used to arrange a convenient date each month.

"Emergency social meetings" are also scheduled when Perl Mongers from out of town come to visit.
