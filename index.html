Welcome to NY.pm

or as we like to say /New York Perl M((o|u)ngers|aniacs)*/

Perl Mongers is a loose confederation of Perl hackers, coders, and accidental programmers whose goal is to develop the social community of Perl - after all, it's more than a language - it's a culture and a floor wax.

New York Perl Mongers is oldest Perl Mongers group in the world, created by brian d foy in 1997 at the First O'Reilly Perl Conference (which later went on to become OSCON).

Socal Meetings

NY.pm runs social meetings meetings approximately once a month. We have been doing so for over 10 years, and you are warmly invited to attend.

There is no set meeting schedule: we use a mailing list to arrange a convenient date each month.

Join the NY.pm mailing list now to make sure you don't miss our next meeting!

Technical Meetings

Perl Seminar NY runs monthly technical workshops for Perl programmers and users in New York City. To learn more, visit the Perl Seminar NY site.
